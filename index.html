Aim

The UL Developer Society, affectionally referred to as UL Dev Society, is an initiative run by students for students for students.UL Dev aims to support their members, both academically and socially, by providing them with a variety of events to enrich their skills.

Vision

To see an environment at UL where bursaries, internships & junior roles are more accessible to UL student

About Us

UL Developer Society is a team, that is determined to help the students of Limpopo to gain the necessary skills to break into the IT sector of South Africa. We help students locate new Opportunities such as bursaries and internships.
